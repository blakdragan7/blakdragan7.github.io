IEEE Competition Robot

The first project I worked on after joining Devry. I developed an AI for a robot that was used in the IEEE competition of that year. I created an algorithm that was able to determine color, size and distance of blocks that then sent commands to a beagleboard XM that controlled servo's and steppers that moved the robot and made it pick up and place blocks based on that information.



OpenGL 4.4 Game Engine

I created a Game Engine that used OpenGL 4.4. written in C++. It used a math library I created for physics and rendered meshes that were loaded in from Callada (.dae) files. It supported skeletal animations and post processing effects. It also supported a multitude of shader effect per rendered objects that I created in GLSL (OpenGL Shader Language)..

Huebble Watchapp for Pebble Smartwatch

Created a watch app for the pebble watch in C that communicated with a phone via bluetooth. This app contolled pebble hue lights via there web API. It had a multitude of options including turning the light on and off, a "party mode" that made the lights change to a random color when you shook your wrist and other features. There is a video explaining it here.



Doom Watch Face for Pebble Smartwatch

Created a watch face for Pebble watch called doom. A watchface is essentially a cool way to tell time on the pebble smart watch. My watch face is modeled after doom and shows the doom guy destroying an imp every minute while keeping track of seconds from the face looking back and forth. Here is a video explaining it here.
