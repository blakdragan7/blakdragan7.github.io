IEEE Competition Robot

The first project I worked on after joining Devry. I develpoed AI for a robot that was used in the IEEE competition of that year. I created an algoritihm that was able to determine color , size and sitance of blocks that then sent commands to a beagle board that controled serveros and steppers that moved the robot and made it pick up and place such blocks.



OpenGL 4.4 Game Engine

I created a Game Engine that used OpenGL 4.4. written in C++. It used a math library I created for physics and rendered Meshes that were loaded in from Callada (.dae) files. It supported Skeleatal animations and Post processing effects. It also supported a multitued of shader effect per rendered objects that I created in glsl..

Doom Watch Face for Pebble Smartwatch

Created a watch face for Pebble watch called doom. A watchface is essentially a cool way to tell time on the pebble smart watch. My watch face is modeled after doom and shows the doom guy destroying an imp every minute while keeping track of seconds from the face looking back and forth. There is a video explaining it here.
